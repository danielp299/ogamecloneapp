# Astrophysics

Allows colonization of new planets and finding more on expeditions.

## Technical Details
- **Base Cost:** Metal: 4,000, Crystal: 8,000, Deuterium: 4,000
- **Base Duration:** 40m
- **Cost Scaling:** 1.75
- **Requirements:** Research Lab (Level 3), Espionage Technology (Level 4), Impulse Drive (Level 3)

## Functionality
Determines the maximum number of planets you can colonize.
**Max Planets Formula:** `1 + (Astrophysics Level + 1) / 2`
