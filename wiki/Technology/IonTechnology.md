# Ion Technology

Required for Ion Cannons and Cruisers.

## Technical Details
- **Base Cost:** Metal: 1,000, Crystal: 300, Deuterium: 100
- **Base Duration:** 15m
- **Requirements:** Research Lab (Level 4), Laser Technology (Level 5), Energy Technology (Level 4)

## Functionality
Prerequisite for the Ion Cannon defense and Plasma Technology.
