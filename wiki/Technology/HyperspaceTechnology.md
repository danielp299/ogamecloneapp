# Hyperspace Technology

Allows travel through hyperspace dimensions.

## Technical Details
- **Base Cost:** Crystal: 4,000, Deuterium: 2,000
- **Base Duration:** 20m
- **Requirements:** Research Lab (Level 7), Energy Technology (Level 5), Shielding Technology (Level 5)

## Functionality
Required for advanced propulsion systems (Hyperspace Drive) and the Intergalactic Research Network.
