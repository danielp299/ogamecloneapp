# Plasma Technology

Required for Plasma Turrets and Bombers.

## Technical Details
- **Base Cost:** Metal: 2,000, Crystal: 4,000, Deuterium: 1,000
- **Base Duration:** 30m
- **Requirements:** Research Lab (Level 4), Energy Technology (Level 8), Laser Technology (Level 10), Ion Technology (Level 5)

## Functionality
Prerequisite for the Plasma Turret defense.
