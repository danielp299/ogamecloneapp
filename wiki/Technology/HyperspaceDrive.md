# Hyperspace Drive

Advanced propulsion for Battleships and Destroyers.

## Technical Details
- **Base Cost:** Metal: 10,000, Crystal: 20,000, Deuterium: 6,000
- **Base Duration:** 45m
- **Requirements:** Research Lab (Level 7), Hyperspace Technology (Level 3)

## Functionality
Increases the speed of ships equipped with hyperspace engines (e.g., Battleship, Destroyer, Death Star).
