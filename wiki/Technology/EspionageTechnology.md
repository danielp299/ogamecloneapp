# Espionage Technology

Information is power. Espionage technology helps you spy on other players.

## Technical Details
- **Base Cost:** Metal: 200, Crystal: 1,000, Deuterium: 200
- **Base Duration:** 2m
- **Requirements:** Research Lab (Level 3)

## Functionality
Determines the amount of information you can see in espionage reports and your ability to detect enemy probes.
