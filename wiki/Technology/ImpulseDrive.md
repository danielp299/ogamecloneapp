# Impulse Drive

Faster propulsion for Cruisers and Heavy Fighters.

## Technical Details
- **Base Cost:** Metal: 2,000, Crystal: 4,000, Deuterium: 600
- **Base Duration:** 12m
- **Requirements:** Research Lab (Level 2), Energy Technology (Level 1)

## Functionality
Increases the speed of ships equipped with impulse engines (e.g., Heavy Fighter, Cruiser, Colony Ship).
