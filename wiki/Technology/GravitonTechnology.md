# Graviton Technology

Required for Death Star. Researched instantly if energy is sufficient.

## Technical Details
- **Base Energy Cost:** 300,000
- **Base Duration:** Instant
- **Cost Scaling:** 3.0
- **Requirements:** Research Lab (Level 12)

## Functionality
A high-level technology required to build the Death Star. Unlike other technologies, it costs Energy instead of resources and completes instantly if the energy requirement is met.
