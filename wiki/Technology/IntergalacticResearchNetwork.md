# Intergalactic Research Network

Links research labs on different planets.

## Technical Details
- **Base Cost:** Metal: 240,000, Crystal: 400,000, Deuterium: 160,000
- **Base Duration:** 2h
- **Requirements:** Research Lab (Level 10), Computer Technology (Level 8), Hyperspace Technology (Level 8)

## Functionality
Allows you to combine the levels of Research Labs on different planets to reduce research time.
