# Combustion Drive

Basic propulsion for ships like Light Fighters and Transporters.

## Technical Details
- **Base Cost:** Metal: 400, Deuterium: 600
- **Base Duration:** 4m
- **Requirements:** Research Lab (Level 1), Energy Technology (Level 1)

## Functionality
Increases the speed of ships equipped with combustion engines (e.g., Small Cargo, Light Fighter, Recycler, Espionage Probe).
