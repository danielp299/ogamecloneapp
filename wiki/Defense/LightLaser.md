# Light Laser (LL)

Uses concentrated light to damage enemies.

## Technical Details
- **Base Cost:** Metal: 1,500, Crystal: 500
- **Base Duration:** 30s
- **Requirements:** Shipyard (Level 2), Laser Technology (Level 3)

## Stats
- **Structure:** 2,000
- **Shield:** 25
- **Attack:** 100

## Functionality
Standard laser defense.
