# Plasma Turret (PT)

The most powerful defensive structure.

## Technical Details
- **Base Cost:** Metal: 50,000, Crystal: 50,000, Deuterium: 30,000
- **Base Duration:** 10m
- **Requirements:** Shipyard (Level 8), Plasma Technology (Level 7)

## Stats
- **Structure:** 100,000
- **Shield:** 300
- **Attack:** 3,000

## Functionality
Elite defense unit capable of damaging even the largest ships.
