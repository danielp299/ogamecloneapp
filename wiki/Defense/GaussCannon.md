# Gauss Cannon (GC)

Fires high-velocity projectiles.

## Technical Details
- **Base Cost:** Metal: 20,000, Crystal: 15,000, Deuterium: 2,000
- **Base Duration:** 2m 30s
- **Requirements:** Shipyard (Level 6), Energy Technology (Level 6), Weapons Technology (Level 3)

## Stats
- **Structure:** 35,000
- **Shield:** 200
- **Attack:** 1,100

## Functionality
Heavy artillery defense with high damage output.
