# Heavy Laser (HL)

Stronger version of the light laser.

## Technical Details
- **Base Cost:** Metal: 6,000, Crystal: 2,000
- **Base Duration:** 60s
- **Requirements:** Shipyard (Level 4), Laser Technology (Level 6)

## Stats
- **Structure:** 8,000
- **Shield:** 100
- **Attack:** 250

## Functionality
Improved laser defense with better armor and attack power.
