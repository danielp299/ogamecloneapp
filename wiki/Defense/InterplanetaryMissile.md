# Interplanetary Missile (IPM)

Destroys enemy defenses remotely.

## Technical Details
- **Base Cost:** Metal: 12,500, Crystal: 2,500, Deuterium: 10,000
- **Base Duration:** 5m
- **Requirements:** Shipyard (Level 1), Missile Silo (Level 4), Impulse Drive (Level 1)

## Stats
- **Structure:** 15,000
- **Shield:** 1
- **Attack:** 12,000

## Functionality
Used to attack enemy defenses on other planets without engaging in fleet combat.
