# Anti-Ballistic Missile (ABM)

Intercepts interplanetary missiles.

## Technical Details
- **Base Cost:** Metal: 8,000, Deuterium: 2,000
- **Base Duration:** 2m
- **Requirements:** Shipyard (Level 1), Missile Silo (Level 2)

## Stats
- **Structure:** 8,000
- **Shield:** 1
- **Attack:** 1

## Functionality
Intercepts and destroys incoming Interplanetary Missiles.
