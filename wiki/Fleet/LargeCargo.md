# Large Cargo (LC)

A heavy transporter with huge capacity.

## Technical Details
- **Base Cost:** Metal: 6,000, Crystal: 6,000
- **Base Duration:** 50s
- **Requirements:** Shipyard (Level 4), Combustion Drive (Level 6)

## Stats
- **Structure:** 12,000
- **Shield:** 25
- **Attack:** 5
- **Capacity:** 25,000
- **Base Speed:** 7,500
- **Fuel Consumption:** 50

## Functionality
Used to transport large amounts of resources between planets.
