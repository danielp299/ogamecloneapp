# Cruiser (CR)

Fast and dangerous to fighters.

## Technical Details
- **Base Cost:** Metal: 20,000, Crystal: 7,000, Deuterium: 2,000
- **Base Duration:** 2m
- **Requirements:** Shipyard (Level 5), Impulse Drive (Level 4), Ion Technology (Level 2)

## Stats
- **Structure:** 27,000
- **Shield:** 50
- **Attack:** 400
- **Capacity:** 800
- **Base Speed:** 15,000
- **Fuel Consumption:** 300

## Functionality
Very fast ship with rapid fire against fighters.
