# Recycler (REC)

Harvests debris fields.

## Technical Details
- **Base Cost:** Metal: 10,000, Crystal: 6,000, Deuterium: 2,000
- **Base Duration:** 3m
- **Requirements:** Shipyard (Level 4), Combustion Drive (Level 6), Shielding Technology (Level 2)

## Stats
- **Structure:** 16,000
- **Shield:** 10
- **Attack:** 1
- **Capacity:** 20,000
- **Base Speed:** 2,000
- **Fuel Consumption:** 300

## Functionality
Used to harvest resources (debris) from destroyed ships and defenses floating in orbit.
