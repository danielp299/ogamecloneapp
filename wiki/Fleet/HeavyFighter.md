# Heavy Fighter (HF)

Better armored than the light fighter.

## Technical Details
- **Base Cost:** Metal: 6,000, Crystal: 4,000
- **Base Duration:** 40s
- **Requirements:** Shipyard (Level 3), Impulse Drive (Level 2)

## Stats
- **Structure:** 10,000
- **Shield:** 25
- **Attack:** 150
- **Capacity:** 100
- **Base Speed:** 10,000
- **Fuel Consumption:** 75

## Functionality
Stronger combat ship used to counter Light Fighters.
