# Small Cargo (SC)

An agile transporter.

## Technical Details
- **Base Cost:** Metal: 2,000, Crystal: 2,000
- **Base Duration:** 20s
- **Requirements:** Shipyard (Level 2), Combustion Drive (Level 2)

## Stats
- **Structure:** 4,000
- **Shield:** 10
- **Attack:** 5
- **Capacity:** 5,000
- **Base Speed:** 5,000
- **Fuel Consumption:** 10

## Functionality
Used to transport resources between planets.
