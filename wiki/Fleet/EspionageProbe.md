# Espionage Probe (ESP)

Fast drone for spying.

## Technical Details
- **Base Cost:** Crystal: 1,000
- **Base Duration:** 5s
- **Requirements:** Shipyard (Level 3), Combustion Drive (Level 3), Espionage Technology (Level 2)

## Stats
- **Structure:** 1,000
- **Shield:** 0
- **Attack:** 0
- **Capacity:** 5
- **Base Speed:** 100,000,000
- **Fuel Consumption:** 1

## Functionality
Used to gather information on enemy planets (Resources, Defense, Fleet, etc.).
