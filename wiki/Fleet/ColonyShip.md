# Colony Ship (CS)

Used to colonize new planets.

## Technical Details
- **Base Cost:** Metal: 10,000, Crystal: 20,000, Deuterium: 10,000
- **Base Duration:** 5m
- **Requirements:** Shipyard (Level 4), Impulse Drive (Level 3)

## Stats
- **Structure:** 30,000
- **Shield:** 100
- **Attack:** 50
- **Capacity:** 7,500
- **Base Speed:** 2,500
- **Fuel Consumption:** 1,000

## Functionality
Consumed upon successful colonization of a new planet.
