# Crystal Mine

Produces Crystal.

## Technical Details
- **Base Cost:** Metal: 48, Crystal: 24
- **Energy Consumption:** 10 * Level * 1.1^Level
- **Base Duration:** 15s
- **Cost Scaling:** 2.0 (Doubles per level)

## Functionality
Produces Crystal resources.
**Production Formula:** `20 * Level * 1.1^Level` per hour.
