# Deuterium Synthesizer

Produces Deuterium.

## Technical Details
- **Base Cost:** Metal: 225, Crystal: 75
- **Energy Consumption:** 20 * Level * 1.1^Level
- **Base Duration:** 30s
- **Cost Scaling:** 2.0 (Doubles per level)

## Functionality
Produces Deuterium resources.
**Production Formula:** `10 * Level * 1.1^Level` per hour.
